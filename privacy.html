Privacy Policy

Standing for your privacy, standing for Kenya.

1. Introduction

Welcome to the SISI NDIO SIFUNA web app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy informs you of how we handle your personal data when you visit our website or use our application in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect, use, and store different kinds of personal data about you, including:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, or national ID (if provided for verification).
  • Contact Data: Email address and telephone numbers.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and location data.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website or platform.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your data only for lawful purposes, specifically:

  • To register you as a member or supporter of the movement.
  • To communicate updates, newsletters, or political alerts.
  • To improve our platform’s functionality and user experience.
  • To comply with legal or regulatory obligations in Kenya.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under the Data Protection Act, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: You have given us clear consent to process your data for a specific purpose.
  • Legitimate Interest: Necessary for the management of the platform and engagement with supporters.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party IT and administration services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by Kenyan law to prevent fraud or respond to legal processes.

6. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way.

7. Your Legal Rights

As a data subject in Kenya, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure ("The right to be forgotten").
  • Object to processing for direct marketing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
